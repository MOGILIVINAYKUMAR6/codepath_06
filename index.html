Mental Health and the Floundering Informal Worker

Exploring the challenges faced by informal workers and the need for better mental health support.

Learn More

About the Issue

The informal workforce often faces tremendous economic and psychological stress. Mental health is a critical concern that needs to be addressed to improve their quality of life.

Common Issues

Financial Instability

Many informal workers live paycheck to paycheck, adding pressure on mental well-being.

Lack of Support

With no formal mental health support systems, informal workers often suffer in silence.

Workplace Hazards

Informal workers are often exposed to unsafe work environments, contributing to stress and anxiety.

Potential Solutions

We believe that collective efforts can help alleviate the burden on informal workers. Here are some strategies that can help:

  • Provide accessible mental health care services.
  • Introduce financial aid and social safety nets.
  • Promote workplace safety and stress management programs.
